When adding new cultures, these are the changes to make.

Required changes:
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\blankMod\output\localisation\cultures_l_<language>.yml
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\configurables\names.txt
Add culture badge to C:\MyProjects\paradoxGameConvertersForks\Vic2ToHoI4\Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\blankMod\output\gfx\interface\culture
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\blankMod\output\interface\culture_ideas.gfx
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\blankMod\output\common\ideas\_cultures.txt
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\configurables\cultureGroupToGraphics.txt

Optional changes:
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\configurables\OperativeNamesMappings.txt
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\configurables\IntelligenceAgencyLogos.txt
Update Vic2ToHoI4\Data_Files\configurables\CultureToVoiceMappings.txt
